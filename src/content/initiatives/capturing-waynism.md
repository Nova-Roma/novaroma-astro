Wayne Chang’s teaching wove rigorous theory with lived practice in innovation and work-integrated learning. **Capturing Waynism** gathers syllabi, recordings where permissions allow, student artifacts, and alumni reflections so that unfinished threads can be continued in the open.

A curatorial circle works with families and institutions to respect privacy while preserving insight. Parallel research tracks investigate how his questions—on mentorship, failure, and institutional courage—show up in today’s co-op and residency models.

Contributors include former students now leading programmes in health tech, public sector labs, and civic design. The project funds small grants for **continuing inquiry** explicitly tied to themes from Wayne’s archive, with public symposia to share findings.
