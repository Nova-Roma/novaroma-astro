Techniques and examples for achieving balance in digital design, the Swiss way.

We will explore proportion systems, from the golden ratio to modular scales,
and apply them to interface compositions. Live demos will show how subtle
changes in spacing transform perceived harmony and usability.

## Key Takeaways

- Using the golden ratio in layouts
- Achieving harmony with consistent spacing
- Visual balance in typography and imagery
- Communicating proportion decisions to stakeholders
