Universities, crown corporations, and regulated industries often need the same thing: a defensible narrative for what changes now versus later. We run discovery sprints with executives and union stewards in the room so trade-offs are explicit.

Deliverables include decision logs, risk registers, and pilot scopes sized for one academic term or one budget cycle. Where policy gaps exist, we connect legal and procurement partners from the ecosystem.

Engagements typically run twelve to sixteen weeks with executive sponsorship and a named product owner on the client side.
