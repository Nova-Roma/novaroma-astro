## About Swissted

Swissted is a creative collective inspired by Swiss graphic design and modern art. They collaborate with artists and designers to produce exhibitions, workshops, and limited-edition prints that celebrate visual culture.

## What They Support

- Art and design exhibitions
- Creative workshops
- Limited-edition print collaborations
