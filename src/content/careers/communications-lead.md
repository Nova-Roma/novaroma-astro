## Role

You will own editorial calendar planning, campaign copy, and stakeholder updates across email, web, and social. You work closely with programme leads and partners to translate complex initiatives into clear, accessible language.

## You

- Five or more years in communications, journalism, or related fields in non-profit, public sector, or mission-driven organizations.
- Strong writing and editing in English; French is an asset.
- Comfortable coordinating contractors and light multimedia production.

## We offer

Flexible remote-first work within Canada, professional development budget, and a collaborative team focused on evidence-based foresight and cross-sector collaboration.

## How to apply

Send your CV, three writing samples, and a short note on why this mission matters to you to [careers@novaroma.org](mailto:careers@novaroma.org). Use the subject line **Communications Lead**.
