## About Anna

Anna Müller is a Swiss product designer renowned for her minimalist, user-focused approach and her dedication to timeless visual systems. With over a decade of experience, Anna has led multidisciplinary teams at Swiss Digital Studio, where she champions clarity, usability, and the Swiss tradition of design excellence. Her work bridges the gap between classic Swiss principles and modern digital products, resulting in interfaces that are both beautiful and highly functional. Anna is a frequent speaker at international design conferences and a mentor to young designers across Europe.

Her philosophy centers on the belief that design should serve people first, and she is passionate about creating products that are accessible, inclusive, and enduring. Anna’s leadership style is collaborative and empowering, fostering creativity and innovation within her teams.
