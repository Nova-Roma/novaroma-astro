Learn how to use animation to guide users and add delight to your products.

This session dives deep into the choreography of motion for digital products.
Attendees will learn how to storyboard micro-interactions, craft transitions
that support hierarchy, and use rhythm to create a sense of narrative flow.

We will review real-world product launches, highlighting what went well and
where animation choices created friction. Finally, we will explore how to
prototype motion rapidly and test it with users before investing in full
production.

## Key Takeaways

- Principles of UI animation
- Tools and libraries
- Performance best practices
- Evaluating motion for accessibility and inclusivity
