## About BET300 and ECOOP Community

BET300 and the ECOOP program bring students into the practice of building and growing ventures through coursework and work-integrated learning alongside the broader entrepreneurship ecosystem.

## What They Support

- Hands-on venture learning
- Co-op placements with entrepreneurial organizations
- Community connections across campus programs
