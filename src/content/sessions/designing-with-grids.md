A practical session on using grids to achieve clarity and order in digital products.

We will unpack the legacy of Swiss grid theory and translate it into flexible
systems for contemporary product teams. Expect a mix of historical context,
live layout critiques, and hands-on frameworks you can bring back to your
design sprints.

## Key Takeaways

- Understand the fundamentals of grid systems
- Learn how to apply modular grids to web layouts
- See real-world examples of Swiss design grids
- Document grids so that cross-functional teams can adopt them quickly
