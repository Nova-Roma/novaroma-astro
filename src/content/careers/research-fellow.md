## Role

Support the research team in producing concise briefings, literature reviews, and session notes for residencies and roundtables. Fellows may co-author public-facing insights under editorial guidance.

## You

- Graduate degree or equivalent experience in policy, economics, STS, or a related field.
- Demonstrated research and synthesis skills; comfort with mixed methods is a plus.
- Able to work independently with clear deadlines.

## Terms

Twelve-month contract with possibility of extension. Compensation aligned with non-profit fellowship norms; details shared with shortlisted candidates.

## How to apply

Email [careers@novaroma.org](mailto:careers@novaroma.org) with **Research Fellow** in the subject line. Include CV, one writing sample (≤ 10 pages), and two references.
