## About

Francis Wang is a multipreneur with over a decade of experience leading companies in engineering and product roles in both technical and product leadership. He creates and coaches sustainable impact ventures across sustainability, education, and health, and works at the intersection of strategic foresight, AI, design thinking, sustainability, and entrepreneurship to support innovative solutions for greater societal resilience.

Francis’s work is grounded in exploring depth and coherence across multiple domains, unified by the goal of advancing resilient, sustainable outcomes. He approaches entrepreneurship and research as vehicles for connecting theory with large-scale, practical impact. His underlying inquiries span:

**Design and Futures Studies:**  
How can intention guide innovation to achieve outcomes that align with long-term interests?

**Business, Technology, and Entrepreneurship:**  
In what ways can organizations and societal structures be built to support ambitious, enduring initiatives?

**Learning Science and Knowledge Management:**  
How can people be equipped for an abundant future through better understanding and collaboration?

**Sustainable Development**, **Health, Fitness, and Longevity:**  
What sustains these individual and collective processes into the future?

These five domains shape the primary framework through which Francis organizes his knowledge and directs his contributions, ensuring all efforts align with a vision for a cohesive and abundant future.

## Current research and roles

Francis is advancing doctoral-level research on how innovation can be structured for long-term public benefit:

- **University of Calgary (DDes)** — Designing frameworks for globally interconnected, self-reinforcing innovation ecosystems that value long-term benefits and strengthen societal resilience.
- **Golden Gate University (DBA, GenAI)** — Human–AI collaboration, ethical leadership, and preserving human agency and “digital sovereignty” in a post-agentic world.

## Nova Roma Horizon

As **Founder** of the **Nova Roma Horizon Innovation Society**, Francis helps steer the organization’s mission to connect research, practice, and community around a more resilient, sustainable future.
