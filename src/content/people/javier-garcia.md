# Speaker Profile

**Name:** Javier García

**Title:** Frontend Engineer, España Innovación

## About Javier

Javier García is a frontend engineer at España Innovación, specializing in high-performance, accessible web interfaces. With a background in both engineering and design, Javier bridges the gap between technical excellence and user-centered design. He has led teams in building scalable UI systems for international clients and is recognized for his commitment to clarity, efficiency, and maintainable code. Javier is an active member of the web development community, sharing his knowledge through talks, workshops, and open-source contributions. He is passionate about making the web faster and more inclusive for everyone.
