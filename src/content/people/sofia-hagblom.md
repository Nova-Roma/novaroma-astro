# Speaker Profile

**Name:** Sofia Hagblom

**Title:** Visual Designer, Swiss Creative Group

## About Sofia

Sofia Hagblom blends Swiss design principles with modern digital aesthetics. Her work focuses on clarity, balance, and the subtle use of color and space to create memorable digital experiences. Sofia has contributed to award-winning projects for Swiss Creative Group, where she advocates for accessible, user-centered design. She is passionate about the intersection of tradition and innovation in visual communication.

## Topics

- Visual design for digital products
- Swiss minimalism
- Color and space in UI

## Fun Fact

Sofia is an avid photographer and finds inspiration in the geometry of urban landscapes.

## Presentation Outline

- The evolution of Swiss design in digital media
- Minimalism as a tool for clarity
- Case study: Redesigning a classic brand for the web
- Balancing tradition and innovation
- Q&A: Applying Swiss principles to your next project
