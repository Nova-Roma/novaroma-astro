## Role

You will help scope questions, gather and organize sources, draft summaries, and maintain citation libraries. You may assist with session notes, light data cleanup, and slide or document formatting under supervision.

## You

- Undergraduate degree or equivalent experience; coursework or work in policy, social sciences, design research, or related fields.
- Strong attention to detail, clear writing, and comfort with academic or grey literature.
- Familiarity with collaborative docs and basic spreadsheets; qualitative coding experience is welcome but not required.

## We offer

Contract role, fully remote within Canada; professional development support and exposure to cross-sector programmes and partners.

## How to apply

Send your CV and a short writing sample (≤ 5 pages) to [careers@novaroma.org](mailto:careers@novaroma.org). Use the subject line **Research Assistant**.
