The sanctuary is not a accelerator with demo-day pressure—it is deliberate slack. Members receive studio time, lightweight governance templates, and introductions to aligned funders when a concept is ready.

Sessions blend critique circles, tool clinics, and quiet co-working. We prioritize psychological safety: you can shelve a project without stigma and return when timing is right.

Capacity is limited each quarter to keep feedback loops tight. Alumni stay in the guild channel for cross-pollination across sectors.
