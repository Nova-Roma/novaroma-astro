A practical guide to planning and conducting user research for digital products.
The session will cover end-to-end research cycles, from scoping and recruiting
to synthesising findings with distributed teams. Expect frameworks, scripts,
and facilitation tips grounded in Swiss design sensibilities.

## Key Takeaways

- Interview techniques
- Usability testing
- Analyzing findings
- Turning insights into actionable design briefs
