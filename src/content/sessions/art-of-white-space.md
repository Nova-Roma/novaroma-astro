Discover the Swiss approach to minimalism and why less is often more.

We will study galleries of classic poster work, then translate those
principles into interface layouts. Attendees will practise editing UI screens
to remove clutter, using white space to guide attention and create rhythm.

## Key Takeaways

- The importance of negative space in design
- How to create focus and hierarchy with space
- Examples from classic Swiss posters
- Techniques for steering teams towards restraint
