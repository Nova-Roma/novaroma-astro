From inspiring talks to hands-on workshops, the 2025 Swiss Design Summit brought together creative minds to celebrate clarity, order, and innovation.

Keynotes unpacked the legacy of Swiss grids in digital products, showing how modern design systems build on decades of typographic discipline. Afternoon ateliers invited teams to prototype responsive type scales, motion systems, and accessibility audits guided by Swiss mentors.

The highlight for many was the residency showcase. Teams presented week-long collaborations with nonprofits, shipping inclusive booking flows, modular dashboards, and editorial tools infused with Swiss precision. Each cohort left with critiques documented, principles refined, and a renewed belief that design can be both poetic and practical.

If you missed this year’s summit, stay tuned for speaker write-ups and workshop retrospectives on the Dusk journal.
