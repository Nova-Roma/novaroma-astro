# Speaker Profile

**Name:** Martina Rossi

**Title:** UX Researcher, Italia Digitale

## About Martina

Martina Rossi is a UX researcher who fuses Italian creativity with Swiss precision. At Italia Digitale, she leads research initiatives that uncover deep user insights and translate them into elegant, effective solutions. Martina’s work is grounded in empathy, rigorous methodology, and a commitment to cross-cultural design. She has published research on proportion and harmony in user interfaces, and her workshops are popular among designers seeking to elevate their craft. Martina is also a classically trained pianist, drawing inspiration from the harmony and structure of music in her design thinking.

## Topics

- User research methodologies
- Proportion and harmony in UI
- Cross-cultural design

## Fun Fact

Martina is a trained classical pianist and believes music and design share the same principles of harmony.
