Swiss design is as relevant as ever. See how designers are translating its timeless principles into the digital world.

Design systems now function as living manuals inspired by Swiss documentation practices. Token libraries act as modular building blocks, echoing the grids of mid-century posters. Accessibility is paramount; contrast ratios, responsive typography, and inclusive motion are the modern equivalents of legible signage.

For mobile interfaces, Swiss design encourages designers to embrace generous whitespace and typographic rhythm. Layouts that breathe allow users to focus, even on smaller screens. The discipline may be decades old, but its insistence on clarity and purpose feels perfectly suited for today’s fast-moving digital landscape.
