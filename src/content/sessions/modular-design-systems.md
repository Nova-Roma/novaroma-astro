A deep dive into building flexible design systems inspired by Swiss rationality.

Together we will map component taxonomies, tackle versioning, and discuss how
to scale documentation without losing clarity. Expect war stories from product
teams who transitioned from scattered pattern libraries to cohesive systems
that support rapid iteration.

## Key Takeaways

- Principles of modularity in design
- Building reusable UI components
- Maintaining consistency at scale
- Operationalising maintenance rituals across squads
